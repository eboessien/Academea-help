School Fee

The School Fee section allows you to view and manage all academic-related fees for students.

How to Access School Fee Ledger

  1. From the main dashboard, locate and click on the "School Fee" option in the Term Bill menu
  2. You will be presented with a list of students
  3. Use the search box to find a specific student or browse through the list
  4. Click on the student's row to select them and view their fee details

Understanding the School Fee Ledger

The ledger displays the following information for the selected student:

  • Student's name and ID at the top of the ledger
  • Transaction history with dates, receipt numbers, and particulars
  • Debit (charges) and Credit (payments) columns
  • Running balance after each transaction

Note: If you see "No data available in table", this means no transactions have been recorded for this student yet.

Weekly Feeding Fee

The Weekly Feeding Fee section allows you to manage meal payments on a weekly basis.

How to Access Weekly Feeding Fee

  1. From the main dashboard, click on "Feeding Fee - Weekly" in the Term Bill menu
  2. Select a student from the list by searching or browsing
  3. Choose the relevant Academic Year and Term from the dropdown menus
  4. The system will display the weekly feeding fee breakdown

Key Features of Weekly Feeding Fee

  • View feeding fees organized by week
  • See total amount, paid amount, and balance for each week
  • Record payments against specific weeks
  • Track payment history through receipt numbers

Termly Feeding Fee

The Termly Feeding Fee section allows you to manage a bulk payment for meals for the entire term.

How to Access Termly Feeding Fee

  1. From the main dashboard, click on "Feeding Fee - Termly" in the Term Bill menu
  2. Select a student from the list
  3. Choose the appropriate Academic Year and Term from the dropdown menus
  4. The system will display the termly feeding fee information

Key Features of Termly Feeding Fee

  • View the total term feeding fee amount
  • See a single payment record for the entire term
  • Track payments made towards the termly feeding fee
  • Monitor the overall balance for the term
