Academea Help

Top Section: Configuration Panel

This part, titled "Help/Show Columns," acts as a control panel for the table below it.
It allows the user to customize their view by showing or hiding specific columns of information (like Phone, Address, or Qualification).
The lists under "First Name," "Other Names," etc., are likely groups of these customizable columns.

Bottom Section: Staff Data Table

This is the main content of the page, a large table labeled "All Staff" that displays a list of all current staff members.
Each row represents a single employee, and each column contains a specific piece of information about them,
such as their Staff ID, Name, Department, Contact details, and Employment dates.
In short, the top section lets you customize what information you see, and the bottom section displays that information in a detailed list.
