SWAP FEES

Go to the account menu and click on the Swap Fee link. Next key in the receipt of the fee paid that have to credit to another student.

Click on search to display the details of the receipt. Now click on the pencil icon to display the Swap Fee form

Select the class of the student who has to credit with this fee. Now select the name of the student and click on swap to credit the student. Once the swap is complete click on the print icon to print the receipt.
