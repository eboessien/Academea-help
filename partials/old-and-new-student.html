Navigation to Student Management

How to Access Student Modules

Both New Students and Old Students modules can be accessed through the main Students menu in the AcademEA system.

Log in to your AcademEA account with appropriate privileges.

From the main menu, navigate to Students.

Main menu showing Students option

Click on either New Students or Old Students from the student management submenu.

Students submenu showing New Students and Old Students options

You will be directed to the selected student dashboard where you can view and manage student records.

New Students Management

About New Students

The New Students section allows you to manage recently admitted students who have just joined your institution. This module helps you track and organize information for students in their first academic year.

Key functions of the New Students module include:

  • Viewing all newly admitted students
  • Adding new student records to the system
  • Managing student profiles and information
  • Tracking admission progress and documentation

Managing New Students

Viewing New Students

The New Students interface displays a list of all recently admitted students with their key information.

New Students Interface

Main interface for viewing and managing new students

Adding a New Student

Navigate to Students → New Students → Add Student.

Fill in the required student information including personal details, contact information, and academic program.

Upload any necessary documents (identification, previous academic records, etc.).

Click Submit to save the new student record.

Tips for Managing New Students

  • Ensure all required fields are completed for each new student record
  • Use the search function to quickly locate specific students
  • Customize your column view to show only the most relevant information
  • Regularly update student status as they progress through the admission process

Old Students Management

About Old Students

The Old Students section (also referred to as Past Students) allows you to manage records of students who have completed their studies or left your institution. This module helps maintain historical data and alumni information.

Key functions of the Old Students module include:

  • Accessing historical student records
  • Maintaining alumni contact information
  • Tracking student academic history
  • Generating reports on past student performance

Managing Old Students

Viewing Old Students

The Old Students interface displays a comprehensive list of all past students with their complete academic history.

Old Students Interface

Main interface for viewing and managing old students

Searching for Past Students

Use the search box at the top of the Old Students page to find specific students.

You can search by student ID, name, program, or graduation year.

The results will filter in real-time as you type.

Use advanced search options for more specific queries.

Tips for Managing Old Students

  • Regularly update contact information for alumni to maintain communication channels
  • Use the export feature to backup historical student data periodically
  • Create custom views for different alumni groups based on graduation year or program
  • Utilize the search function to quickly locate specific alumni records

Common Features

Hide/Show Columns

Both New Students and Old Students modules feature a customizable column view that allows you to show or hide specific data columns based on your needs.

Click the Hide/Show Columns button in the toolbar.

Check the boxes for columns you want to display; uncheck those you want to hide.

Your preferences will be saved automatically for future sessions.

Available Columns

Personal Information

  • Student ID
  • First Name
  • Middle Name
  • Last Name
  • Gender

Academic Information

  • Program
  • Class
  • Admission Year
  • Year Group

Contact Details

  • Email
  • SMS Number
  • Postal Address
  • Physical Address

Additional Information

  • Nationality
  • Place of Birth
  • Date of Birth
  • Residency Type
  • Date Admitted

Search Functionality

Both modules include a powerful search feature to help you quickly find specific student records.

Locate the search box at the top of the student list.

Type any search term (name, ID, program, etc.).

The results will filter in real-time as you type.

Use advanced search options for more specific queries by clicking the filter icon.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues and Solutions

Issue Possible Cause Solution
Cannot view student records Insufficient permissions or incorrect filters applied Check your user permissions and reset any active filters
Search not returning expected results Incorrect search terms or filters Clear search and filters, then try again with different terms
Columns not displaying correctly Browser cache issues or custom column settings Clear browser cache or reset column preferences
Unable to export data Browser settings or large data set Check browser download settings or try exporting smaller batches
Slow performance Large number of records or system resources Use filters to reduce visible records or contact system administrator
