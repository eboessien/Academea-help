Promote Student

To promote a student to the next class, follow these steps:

Crucial First Step: Dealing with Graduating Students (JHS3)

Before you promote any other classes, you must handle the students who are leaving. The current JHS3 class represents your graduating students. You need to archive them into the "Old Students" database to keep your main student list clean and make space for the new JHS3 class.

How to Move JHS3 to Old Students:

  1. Go to the main menu and click on "Move Students to Old" under the Administration section.
  2. You will see a form with two dropdown menus:
      From Class: Select "JHS 3" from this list. This tells the system which group of students to move. To Year Group: Select the academic year they are graduating in (e.g., "2023/2024"). This archives them under that specific year group for future records.
  3. Click the "Move" button. The system will now transfer all JHS3 students out of the active classes and into the old student database.

How to Promote All Other Classes:

Promotion moves students from one active class to the next (e.g., from JHS 2 to the now-empty JHS 3).

  1. From the main menu, click on "Promote Student"
  2. On the promotion page, you will see another simple form:
      From Class: Choose the class you want to promote from (e.g., JHS 2). To Class: Choose the class you want to promote them to (e.g., JHS 3).
  3. Click the "Promote" button. The system will move all students from the "From Class" into the "To Class".

Repeat this process for each class that needs to be promoted:

JHS 2 -> JHS 3

JHS 1 -> JHS 2

Basic 6 -> JHS 1

etc.

In a nutshell: Always remember to Move JHS3 out first, then Promote all other classes up. This keeps your student lists perfectly organized for the new year.
