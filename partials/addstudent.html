Introduction to Adding Students

The Add Student feature allows you to register new students into the AcademEA system. This process collects all necessary information to create a complete student profile.

Note

You need appropriate permissions to add students. Contact your system administrator if you don't see the Add Student option.

In this guide, you'll learn how to:

  • Navigate to the Add Student section
  • Complete the multi-step form
  • Enter student information
  • Add parent/guardian details
  • Save and complete the process

Navigating to Add Student

To access the Add Student section:

Log in to your AcademEA account and you'll be directed to the Dashboard homepage.

In the sidebar navigation, locate and click on the "Students" menu to expand it.

From the expanded Students menu, click on "Add Student" to open the form.

Form Steps Overview

The Add Student form is divided into multiple steps to make the process organized and manageable:

Student Information: Enter the student's personal details including name, gender, date of birth, and contact information.

Father Information: Provide details about the student's father including name, contact information, occupation and education.

Mother Information: Provide details about the student's mother including name, contact information, occupation and education.

Guardian Information: Provide details about the student's guardian (if different from parents) including relationship to student.

Student Information

The first step requires you to enter the student's personal information. Here's what you need to know:

Student Information Form

Form for entering student information

Tip

Fields marked with an asterisk (*) are required. You must complete these fields to proceed to the next step.

Fill in all fields marked with an asterisk. These are essential for creating the student record.

While optional fields are not required, providing complete information helps create a more comprehensive student profile.

Click the "Next" button to proceed to the Father Information step once all required fields are completed.

Parent Information

The next steps involve entering information about the student's parents or guardians:

Father Information: Complete information about the student's father including full name, mobile number, addresses, education level and occupation.

Mother Information: Complete information about the student's mother including full name, mobile number, addresses, education level and occupation.

Guardian Information: If the student's guardian is different from their parents, provide guardian's full name, mobile number, relationship to the student, and addresses.

Note

You can use the "Previous" button to go back to earlier steps and make changes if needed.

Completing the Process

After completing all form steps, you can finalize the student registration:

Use the "Previous" button to navigate through all steps and verify that all information is correct.

Click the "Save Student" button to complete the registration process.

You should receive a confirmation message indicating the student has been successfully added to the system.

Tip

You can save a partially completed form as a draft and return to it later by clicking the "Save Draft" button.

Important

After saving, you can add additional information like medical details from the Current Students page.

Troubleshooting

Here are solutions to common issues you might encounter:

Issue Possible Cause Solution
Can't proceed to next step Required fields not completed Ensure all required fields (marked with *) are completed
Form not saving Internet connection issues Check your internet connection and try again
Dropdown menus not working Browser compatibility issues Try refreshing the page or using a different browser
