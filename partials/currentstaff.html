Current Staff Documentation

Overview

The Current Staff page provides access to detailed information about all staff members who have been added to the system. From this interface, you can view comprehensive staff profiles and manage staff-related data.

Viewing Staff Details

To view the complete details of a staff member:

Navigate to the Current Staff page from the main menu.

Locate the staff member in the staff list on the left side of the page.

Click on the staff member's name to display their details in the right pane.

Figure 1: Staff details displayed after selecting a staff member

Information Tabs

Each staff profile is organized into multiple tabs containing different categories of information. To access these details:

Select a staff member from the list to view their basic information.

Click on the appropriate tab to view specific categories of information.

Available tabs typically include Personal Information, Contact Details, Employment History, Qualifications, and more.

Figure 2: Information tabs for accessing different staff details

Tip

Different staff roles may have different tabs available. For example, teaching staff will have additional tabs for class and subject information.

Adding Staff Photos

You can add or update staff photos directly from the Current Staff page. There are two methods available: uploading from your device or capturing with your camera.

Accessing the Photo Upload Feature

Select the staff member from the list whose photo you want to add or update.

Navigate to the Personal Information tab if not already selected.

Scroll down to the bottom of the page to find the photo section.

Figure 3: Photo upload section in the Personal Information tab

Upload from Device

Click on the "Choose File" button in the photo section.

Browse your device to locate the image file you want to use.

Select the image file and confirm your selection.

A preview will be shown to ensure you've selected the correct image.

Save changes to apply the new photo to the staff profile.

Capture with Camera

Click the "Start Camera" button in the photo section.

Allow the system access to your device's camera when prompted.

Position the staff member within the camera frame.

Click "Take Photo" to capture the image.

Click "Stop Camera" when finished.

Save changes to apply the new photo to the staff profile.

Figure 4: Photo upload interface with device and camera options

Important Notes

• For best results, use a clear, recent photo with a neutral background
• The system may automatically resize or crop images to fit the profile picture format
• Some devices may require additional permissions for camera access
