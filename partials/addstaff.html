Add Staff Documentation

Overview

This documentation explains how to add new staff members to the AcademEA system. The process involves a multi-step form to capture all necessary information about the staff member.

Accessing the Add Staff Feature

To add a staff member, click on the "Add Staff" option in the main menu:

Figure 1: Add Staff menu location

This will open the Add Staff form where you can enter the staff member's details.

Figure 2: Add Staff form interface

Form Steps

The Add Staff form consists of four steps that guide you through the process of entering staff information:

Figure 3: The four steps of the Add Staff form

Step 1: Personal Information - Enter basic details like name, date of birth, gender, etc.

Step 2: Education & Contact Information - Add educational background and contact details.

Step 3: Class and Subject - Specify which classes and subjects the staff teaches (for teaching staff).

Step 4: Other Information - Add additional details like medical information, employment history, etc.

Adding Class and Subject Information

To add the class and subject taught by a staff member:

Complete the necessary information under the Personal Information and Education & Contact tabs first.

Navigate to the Class and Subject tab.

Select the class the staff teaches. All subjects related to that class will be displayed.

Select the subject(s) that the staff teaches. The selected class and subject will appear in the right pane.

To remove a subject from the staff's list, click the delete icon next to it.

Figure 4: Adding subjects taught by a staff member

Form Navigation

The form includes navigation buttons to move between steps and manage the data entry process:

Figure 5: Form navigation buttons

The navigation options include:

  • Previous: Return to the previous step
  • Next: Move to the next step (only available when all required fields are completed)
  • Save: Save the currently entered data
  • Reset: Clear the form and start over

Important Note

You must complete all required fields (marked with an asterisk *) before you can proceed to the next step.

Figure 6: Required fields marked with asterisks

Saving Information

When sufficient information about the staff member has been entered, the Save button becomes active, allowing you to save the data without completing all steps.

Use the Previous button to return to earlier steps if you need to review or modify information.

Click Save to store the currently entered information. You can return later to complete any missing details.

Use Reset to clear the form and start over if needed.

Tip

You can save partially completed forms and return to them later. This is useful when you need to gather additional information before completing the staff record.
