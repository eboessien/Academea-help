Login Process & Dashboard Overview

Login Process

Accessing the AcademEA system requires a secure login process with two-factor authentication for enhanced security.

Go to the Login Page: Open your web browser and navigate to qasaccounts.academea.net.

Enter Your Credentials: Type in your registered email address and password in the appropriate fields.

Figure 1: Login page interface

Receive an OTP: After entering your credentials, the system will send a One-Time Password (OTP) to your registered email or phone number.

Figure 2: OTP notification example

Enter the OTP: Check your email or phone for the OTP and enter it in the provided field on the login screen.

Figure 3: OTP entry screen

Click "Verify": After entering the correct OTP, click the verify button to complete the authentication process.

Access Granted: If all credentials are correct, you'll be redirected to your account dashboard.

Figure 4: User dashboard after successful login

Security Note

The OTP (One-Time Password) is valid for a limited time only. If it expires, you can request a new one by clicking the "Resend OTP" button.

Dashboard Overview

The dashboard is your central hub for accessing all features and information within the AcademEA system. It provides an overview of key metrics and quick access to important functions.

Figure 5: Complete dashboard view

Financial Summary Section

The dashboard includes a comprehensive financial summary section that displays key metrics about your institution's financial performance.

Figure 6: Financial summary section

This section displays:

Enrollment Metrics

Total number of enrolled students and new admissions for the current period.

Fee Collections

Breakdown of fee collections including daily, monthly, and term totals.

Outstanding Balances

Summary of unpaid fees and outstanding balances across all students.

Feeding Fees

Tracking of feeding fee payments and outstanding amounts.

Tip

Hover over any metric in the financial summary to see more detailed information or click to view the complete report.

Navigation Menu

The left-side navigation menu provides access to all major sections of the AcademEA system, serving as the main control panel for platform features.

Figure 7: Navigation menu

The navigation menu includes access to:

Student Management

Manage student records, admissions, classes, and academic performance.

Staff Management

Handle staff records, assignments, payroll, and performance tracking.

Financial Records

Access fee collections, expenses, financial reports, and accounting functions.

Reports & Analytics

Generate various reports on academic performance, finances, and operations.

Messaging

Communicate with parents, staff, and students through SMS and other channels.

System Settings

Configure system preferences, user permissions, and institutional settings.

Note

The options available in the navigation menu may vary based on your user role and permissions within the system.
